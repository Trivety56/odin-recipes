Sloppy Joes

Description

Who doesn't like a Sloppy Joe? They're a fun mess with tons of flavor.

This recipe lets you bring a classic to your table. Don't forget to get chips!

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped green bell peppers
  • 3/4 cup of ketchup
  • 1/2 tsp of garlic powder
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Hamburger Buns

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Cook ground beef for about 3 to 4 minutes.
  2. Add onions and bell peppers to skillet and cook until they are softened, 3-5 minutes
  3. Add ketchup and spices to skillet and stir. Let skillet simmer on low heat for 20-30 minutes.
  4. Portion meat onto the hamburger buns.
